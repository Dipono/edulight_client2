Designed to intensify our efforts in the career development space, the Edulight mentorship programme affords pupils an opportunity to tap into their powers, develop themselves, and move fluidly to higher education environment.


The aim is to mentor learners in their respective career choices by pairing them with mentors that are affiliated in those specific career fields. This is to ensure that learners are groomed well in alignment with their career choices and are left fed with information they will need in future; in grasping opportunities in those career fields.

We believe with the right person by his/her side, a pupil will be well equipped to achieve way more than they aimed for. They will gain knowledge on how to conduct themselves as students, make informed decisions towards their future, and have confidence to take decisive actions that will lead them to greatness. This is made possible by our network of university/college students and graduates, who take on the mentor hat and commit to mentoring our pupils until they finish high school.

Ours is to enable change by shaping the future of young aspiring professionals. All volunteers and learners form an integral part of this mission. So go on and sign up, we need you.
